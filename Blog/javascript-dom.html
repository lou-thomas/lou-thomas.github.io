HTML and CSS - in the animal kingdom

If we wanted to David Attenborough up in here for a mo, we might liken HTML to a millipede. Everyone of those endless legs are in just the right place to make the whole animal make sense and move forward. It's structure is very important, although all those legs are not really made for running, just slithering at a steady pace. CSS however would be more like a leopard. Much faster than HTML and who can deny that leopard print is not the ultimate in fashion and style. When CSS is in town, everyone's gonna be looking their best.
